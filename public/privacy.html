Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

tChartXYZ Bot collects the following types of information:

1.1 Telegram and Discord Data

  • Group/Server Information: Group/server IDs, names, and basic settings
  • User Information: User IDs, usernames, and role information for administrators
  • Bot Configuration: Chart settings, auto-posting preferences, and customizations

1.2 Web Interface Data

  • Configuration Data: Chart settings, image preferences, and customization options
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve service quality

1.3 Technical Data

  • Logs: Server logs for debugging and service improvement
  • Performance Metrics: Response times and error rates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the tChartXYZ Bot service
  • Generating and customizing cryptocurrency charts
  • Managing auto-posting schedules and configurations
  • Improving service functionality and user experience
  • Providing customer support and troubleshooting
  • Ensuring service security and preventing abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with the following measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS
  • Secure server infrastructure with access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

3.2 Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your bot remains active in groups/servers
  • You continue to use our services
  • Required for legal or regulatory compliance

You can request data deletion by contacting us directly.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our service
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, user information may be transferred

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Control

  • Access your stored configuration data
  • Modify or delete your bot settings
  • Export your configuration data
  • Request deletion of your data

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Control auto-posting frequency
  • Choose which channels receive automated posts
  • Opt out of certain features

6. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third-party platforms:

6.1 Telegram

We use Telegram's Bot API to provide bot functionality. Please review Telegram's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

6.2 Discord

We use Discord's Bot API to provide bot functionality. Please review Discord's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

6.3 Blockchain Data Providers

We access public blockchain data through various APIs to generate charts. This data is publicly available and does not contain personal information.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our web interface may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze usage patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending notifications through our bot services for significant changes

11. Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
  • Take immediate steps to contain and investigate the breach
  • Implement additional security measures to prevent future breaches
  • Cooperate with relevant authorities as required

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Telegram: @tChartXYZ_bot
  • X.com: @tChartxyz

13. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, we process your data based on:

  • Contract: To provide the services you requested
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For optional features and communications
